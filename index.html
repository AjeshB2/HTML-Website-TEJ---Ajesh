Home Page

Computer devices are increasingly becoming more essential for modern life. From smart phones to desktop computers people around the world use these devices for work, education, fun, and more. However, we sometimes don't realize that these devices often do more harm than good to the environment. This website will explain the negative impacts of computer production, use, and disposal on the environment. It will also provide ways to reduce the environmental impact of using, producing and disposing of computer devices. These two categories can be accessed through the two buttons in the navigation bar above.
